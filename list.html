Planet Earth🌏- Quick Guide

Earth is a rocky planet that is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet that supports life. 🌍

How To Protect Our Earth🌍

The steps to protect our planet Earth🌍

  1. Reduce, reuse, and recycle waste.
  2. Save water and electricity.
  3. Plant more trees.
  4. Use public transport or walk when possible.
  5. Avoid littering and pollution.

Climate Change🌍

Climate Change
Long-term changes in Earth's temperature and weather patterns.
Global Warming
The increase in Earth's average temperature due to greenhouse gases.
Greenhouse Gases
Gases such as carbon dioxide that trap heat in the atmosphere.
Effects
Rising sea levels, extreme weather, and melting glaciers.
Solutions
Reducing emissions, using renewable energy, and protecting forests.
